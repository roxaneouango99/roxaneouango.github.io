Hi!
I'm Roxane

I am an Electrical and Computer Engineering major at Cornell University. I aim to become an Onco-Dermatologist. I am interested in improving cancer patients' quality of life by preventing and treating dermatological issues arising from cancer treatments' side effects.

My current project TuDermis aims to bring awareness to skin-related issues and connect low-income communities with local dermatologists. I aim for TuDermis to become an integral tool in both medical education and research.

In my free time, I enjoy reading and trying new recipes when I am not thinking of engineering the human body.
